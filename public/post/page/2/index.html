Federal Funds Hike

Sun, Apr 16, 2017 Writing Finance, Interest Rates

This article first appeared in the Reading University Investment Society’s April edition on April 16th, 2017. Last month, the Federal Reserve raised the short-term interest rate target range by 0.25%, to a range of 0.75% to 1%. This is in line with the central bank’s “slow and steady” approach to rate increases after a decade of historically low interest rates, and is the third such rate increase since June of 2006.
